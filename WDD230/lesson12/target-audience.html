Audience

People who like to do their homework before getting to the island, and others looking for information about the services, requirements, and rental process of motor scooters, jeeps, and side-by-sides ATVs on the island of Cozumel off the coast of Mexico in the western Caribbean Sea.

Scenarios:

  • What kind of vehicle can I rent in Cozumel?
  • How many people can go in each vehicle?
  • How much does it cost to rent each vehicle?
  • What is the best fit for my travelling situation?
  • What are the characteristics of each vehicle?
