Site Name:

The site name will be Scoots. The user will find here friendly and easy to rent services of motor scooters, jeeps, and side-by-sides ATVs on the island of Cozumel off the coast of Mexico in the western Caribbean Sea.

Purpose:

The site purpose is to help locals, walk-in tourists, enthusiasts, and cruise-based touring groups or partnerships find friendly and easy to rent services of motor scooters, jeeps, and side-by-sides ATVs by promoting the rental services for those customers that like to do their homework before getting to the island and to provide information about the services, requirements, and rental process.
