Overview

Purpose

This webpage's purpose is to simplify your meal preparation. Here you are going to find ideas about what to cook each day of the week;
you can set your preferences and the ideas will be customized, and my favorite feature, you will be able to input which ingredients
you have in your fridge and the computer will do the magic to tell you different options to cook with those!

Audience

This page is intended to help anyone responsible of preparing a meal. It could be a person who is tired of thinking about what to eat each day
a person who would like to organize the meal prep better, someone tired of always eating the same meals, or someone who doesn't know what to prepare with
the ingredients available at home.
