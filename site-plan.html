Purpose

The Temple Inn & Suites is a hotel chain that builds and manages specialized hotel accommodations near temple locations of The Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-day Saints around the world.

Audience

This page would be useful for those looking for a place to stay while spending time in the temple. Would be an easy-to-use page for anyone interested.
